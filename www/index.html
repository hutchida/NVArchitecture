Lanzarote - Madrid - London

Truly sustainable low impact design

On the island of Lanzarote in the Canary Islands where our studio is based, we are fortunate to have such a variety of local architecture still in use. Truly sustainable design is rarely realised in laboratories producing the most technologically advanced materials. Instead, tried and tested methods evolve over time through communities working with the materials they have available in their location. It is this emphasis on place that we seek to centre in all our projects, whilst realising the real constraints of cost.
